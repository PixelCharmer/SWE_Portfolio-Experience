About Me

Hi, I'm Kari Alcoset, even though I am new to the field I am a passionate front-end developer with a knack for creating beautiful and user-friendly web experiences. What fuels my passion for front-end development is the ever-evolving nature of the industry.

I find joy in tackling complex challenges, researching the latest trends, and creating seamless digital experiences that users love. I believe that every design challenge is an opportunity for creativity and innovation.

During my 14-year tenure in the recruitment industry, I have honed a valuable set of transferable soft skills that I am eager to bring to this career transition. These skills include leadership, adaptability, effective time management, problem-solving, analytical reasoning, critical thinking, and collaborative planning.

When I'm not learning code and refining those skills you can find me at my sewing machine sewing a varitry of different projects from quilts to bowl cozies, experimenting with new cooking recipes, or watching moview with my husband. I believe these diverse experiences bring creativity and a fresh perspective to my work.

I'm always open to exciting new projects and collaborations. Feel free to browse through my portfolio to see some of my recent work, or connect with me on LinkedIn to start a conversation about how we can work together to bring your web development vision to life.
